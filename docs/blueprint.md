# **App Name**: Impala Abastos

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Eye-catching title, subtitle, image related to fresh produce, and a clear call-to-action button.
- About Impala: Overview of Impala Abastos highlighting its role as a key food distribution center for Pereira and the surrounding region, emphasizing its importance for traders, farmers, and end consumers.
- Product Showcase: Visual section with cards or icons displaying the main product categories sold at Impala, such as fruits, vegetables, grains, meats and dairy.
- Why Buy at Impala: Section listing the key benefits of buying at Impala Abastos: high quality products, competitive prices, direct support for local producers, wide variety and constant supply.
- Location and Hours: Display Impala Abastos' exact address, opening hours, and embed a Google Maps to help visitors find the location.
- Regional Impact: Details on how Impala Abastos contributes to regional food security, job creation, and local trade.
- Final Call to Action: Encourage users to visit Impala and support local businesses.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FFA500) to reflect energy, warmth, and the freshness of produce.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), providing a neutral and natural backdrop to emphasize the products.
- Accent color: Yellow-orange (#FFB347) for interactive elements and CTAs, creating contrast and drawing attention.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a modern, legible humanist sans-serif that combines a contemporary look with approachability.
- Use of natural and friendly icons for each product category.
- Responsive layout ensuring the website is accessible and visually appealing on all devices (desktops, tablets, and smartphones).
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to keep users engaged without being distracting.
